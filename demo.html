2D GAME DEMO

Here is the semi-completed version of the 2D mock-up game. You can move the cue around, change direction and even change the speed of your shot. I haven't set up the pockets or a scoring system yet. That will be done soon!

Controls

  • a: move cue left
  • s: move cue down
  • d: move cue right
  • w: move cue up
  • o: rotate cue counter clockwise
  • p: rotate cue clockwise
  • x: hit white ball

Hit 1 - 9 on your keyboard to select your shot power. (9 is default)

Unfortunately your browser does not support the canvas element. Please upgrade to the latest versions of Firefox, Safari, Opera or Chrome
